A Nosey way to Drink a Breakfast

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2oz Bacon Infused Bushmills Irish Whiskey
  • 1/2oz Lemon Juice, fresh
  • 1/2oz Orange Juice, fresh
  • 1/2oz Grade B Maple Syrup
  • 1 Egg

Steps

  1. Add all ingredients to shaker,
    Energetically dry-shake for 15 seconds
  2. Add IOce and shake until well chilled
  3. Strain into Highball Glass

Bacon Infused Whiskey

  1. Cook 8oz Bacon until Fat Rendered
  2. Set Bacon Aside and Pour Drippings into Jar
  3. Add 6oz Bushmills Irish Whiskey and Shake Well
  4. Let Stand in a Cool Place for 48 Hours
  5. Place in Freezer for 30 min to Solidify Fat
  6. Scoop Out Fat and Strain
  7. Enjoy Bacon Irish Whiskey!
