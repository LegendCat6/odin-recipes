The Eggiest Nog to Fog a Wog

Ingredients

  • 2 cups (500mL) Cream
  • 1/2 cup (100g) Sugar
  • 2 Egg
  • 1 cup (250mL) Rum
  • 1/2 cup (125mL) Whisky
  • Garnish: Nutmeg

Steps

  1. Whip 1 cup o' cream in a bowl
  2. Beat Eggs, drizzle in Alcohol slowly, mix until incorporated
    !!!DON'T STOP MIXING: EGGS WILL CURDLE!!!
  3. Mix in sugar, un-whipped cream, and whipped cream until smooth
  4. Transfer to gas escape-able vessel and sit for 3 ish weeks
  5. Enjoy!
