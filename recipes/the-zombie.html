The Zombie Rum Cocktail

A Zombie to make you Mombie

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2oz Jamaican Rum
  • 1 1/2oz Puerto Rican Gold Rum
  • 1oz 151-Proof Demerara Rum
  • 3/4oz Lime Juice, Fresh
  • 1/2oz Don's Mix
  • 1/2oz Falernum
  • 1tsp Grenadine
  • 4 Dashes Pernod
  • 1 Dash Angostura Bitters
  • Garnish: Mint Sprig

Steps

  1. Add all Ingredients (minus Garnish) to a blender with 6oz Crushed Ice
  2. Blend at High Speed for less than 5 seconds
  3. Pour into tall glass or Tiki Mug and add crushed ice to fill
  4. Garnish with Mint Sprig

Don's Mix

  1. Bring 3 Crushed Cinnamon Sticks, 1 Cup Sugar, 1 Cup Water to Boil
  2. Stir until Sugar is Dissolved and Simmer for 2 Minutes
  3. Remove from heat, cool for 2 hours
  4. Strain into Glass Bottle
  5. Add 1 part syrup to 2 parts Fresh Grapefruit Juice
  6. Cover and Keep Refrigerated for up to 2 Weeks
