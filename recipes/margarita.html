The Classic Margarita can turn any Day into a fun Night

Ingredients

  • 2oz White Tequila
  • 1/2oz Orange Liqueur
  • 1oz Fresh Lime Juice
  • 1/2oz Agave Syrup

Garnish

  • Lime Wheel
  • Kosher Salt

Steps

  1. Rim Glass with Kosher Salt
  2. Add ingredients and ice to cocktail shaker,
    Shake until chilled
  3. Stain over rocks glass
  4. Garnish with lime
