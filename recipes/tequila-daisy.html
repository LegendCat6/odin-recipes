Tequila Daisy

Hi, I'm Daisy!

Ingredients

  • 2oz Tequila
  • 1/2oz Grand Marnier
  • 1/2oz Fresh Lemon Juice
  • 1/2tsp SuperFine Sugar
  • Chilled Club Soda

Steps

  1. Shake Lemon Juice and Sugar
  2. Add Tequila, fill with ice
  3. Shake until well chilled
  4. Strain into cocktail glass
  5. Top with club soda
